--- mt_id: 4 layout: post title: The web 2.0 validator date: 2006-01-14 01:38:39 -05:00 --- The dudes at 30 second rule have coded this very kool consensual web 2.0 validator. You can add your own rule via a del.icio.us bookmark. I have added a rule to check for .rail extensions which would indicate use of the MonoRail engine. MonoRail is an open source MVC framework for .NET inspired in the successful Ruby on Rails. Recently i have started using MonoRail in one of my company's projects and have found it a very productive framework that promotes simplicity and separation of concerns. After being introduced to it you will find that the Webforms idea is actually bloated and introduces too much complexity.
